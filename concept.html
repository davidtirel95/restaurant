Everyday trying to change our ineherited social habits. Café Bidule stands against all forms of oppression.

We’re a safe creative open source space for those whose gender, sexuality, color, beliefs or backgrounds are labelled and bullied by a system. We aim to share so we offer a wide range of events such as live music, movie screenings, talks and readings to heigthen the possibilites to share kindness, laughs and knowledge around good coffee, tasty food and amazing drinks.

Most of our food comes from local farmers that we’ve worked with since the beginning of our journey. We also run an organic farm 10kms from here who employs anyone in need that strive to earn a living. Everyone gets a decent pay, food and housing are free such as the help from our social workers.

Whether hanging out at the café or taking care of the events, being apart of Café Bidule is a political statement that helps everyone holisticly.
